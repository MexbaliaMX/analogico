# YouTube Playlists for Physics & Electronics

## Core Physics Foundations
- **MIT OpenCourseWare – Physics I: Classical Mechanics** — full lecture series by Prof. Walter Lewin; vectors, kinematics, energy conservation. https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLUl4u3cNGP60uVBMaoNERc6knT_MgPKS0
- **Feynman Lectures on Physics (Project Tuva)** — curated clips where Richard Feynman explains fundamental concepts like electromagnetism and quantum behavior. https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLcVgsnzNAgwd3TjFnk6eJjF1ITSZRyVbA
- **MinutePhysics** — concise animations covering counterintuitive topics (relativity, wave interference) in digestible episodes. https://www.youtube.com/user/minutephysics

## Electronics & Circuit Design
- **All About Circuits – Fundamentals** — introductory tutorials on Ohm’s law, Kirchhoff rules, and semiconductor basics. https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLo7BXjgCzQkf6ZX6mE6xPD58Ll35vj0X2
- **Afrotechmods** — approachable explanations of analog components, op-amp behavior, and common circuit pitfalls. https://www.youtube.com/user/Afrotechmods
- **GreatScott! Analog Electronics Series** — hands-on builds exploring filters, amplifiers, and power electronics with lab demonstrations. https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLAROrg3NQn7cckNH9bB1hHBwo2o2556Yw

## Electromagnetics & RF
- **Stanford EE 142/242 RF Systems** — lecture recordings on transmission lines, Smith charts, and power amplifiers. https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLoROMvodv4rMC6zfYmnD7UG3LVvwaITY5
- **W2AEW Oscilloscope & RF Tutorials** — practical demonstrations on vector network analyzers, impedance matching, and measurement tricks. https://www.youtube.com/user/w2aew
- **Michael Ossmann – Software Defined Radio Academy** — deep dives into SDR hardware, mixing theory, and spectral analysis. https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL1xpzrG0bJeE5ZTwwl-dg2rt7NmBGtMzh

## Semiconductor Devices & Memories
- **NanoFab Lab at PSU** — cleanroom tours and process walkthroughs for CMOS, MEMS, and resistive memories. https://www.youtube.com/user/nanofablabatpsu
- **EEVblog – Semiconductor Physics** — detailed tear-downs explaining transistor operation, failure modes, and packaging. https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLECEw2VXaYqvqCVFKwHVDA6j_Xt0uDLYE
- **IEEE IEDM Conference Channel** — keynotes and tutorials on emerging memories (RRAM, MRAM) and device scaling challenges. https://www.youtube.com/c/IEEE-EDS

## Supplementary Math & Simulation
- **3Blue1Brown – Essence of Linear Algebra** — visual intuition for vectors, transformations, and eigenvalues. https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLZHQObOWTQDMsr9K-rj53DwVRMYO3t5Yr
- **LTspice Tutorials by Analog Devices** — guided simulations covering AC analysis, transient response, and behavioral modeling. https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLliu5kKXkELS9fF5wgGRFkEy_AViTDIbx
- **Control Systems Lectures (Brian Douglas)** — clear explanations of feedback, stability, and Bode plots, vital for analog loop design. https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLUMWjy5jgHK1NC52DXXrriwihVrYZKqjk
